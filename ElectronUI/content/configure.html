Change alert email addresses, previously entered list will be deleted (comma separated)

Enter a new exclusions list below, this will delete the previous list. It is recommended to keep a text file of your exclusions for any configuration changes.

Example:
Alert 1: "This is an example of an alert from cmd.exe launching Asus controller"
Alert 2: "This is another exmplae of an alert from cmd.exe executing Asus controller.exe"

Setting an exclusion of "launching Asus Controller" will prevent alert 1 from generating but not alert 2.
Setting an exclusion of "Asus Controller" will prevent both alerts from generating.

WARNING: This will prevent SnitchIO from generating alerts that contain these exclusion keywords, anywhere in the alert, exclusively. Alerts are case sensitive.
